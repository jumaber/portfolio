About

I am a Berlin-based UX/UI Developer and former founder of Artconnect.com, the largest online creative community in Berlin with 35k monthly active users. In 2020, I sold my venture and embarked on my true passion: UX, UI Design and Front-end development.

I am currently working as a UX Design tutor at CareerFoundry and freelancing for companies such as Mister Spex, Setting.io, Duxer (Ikea, Userzoom), and creating a product from scratch with KEA. On the side, I organize Design+Friends, a monthly Berlin Designer meetup.
