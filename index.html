About

I am a Berlin-based Product Experience Designer and former founder of Artconnect.com, Berlin's largest online creative community with 35k monthly active users. In 2020, I sold my venture and embraced my true passion: Product Experience Design and Front-end Development.

Currently, I am a Senior Experience Designer at Mister Spex, enhancing the lens configuration user experience.

With a background in Fine Arts and over 10 years of experience in product design, communications strategy, and management, I bring a balanced blend of structure and creativity to diverse teams. Founding a business has given me unique insights into complex projects and the factors influencing their success. It has also taught me to be resourceful and innovative in finding customer-centric solutions. My technical education provides me with a deep understanding of building user-friendly and developer-friendly websites.

In my free time, you will find me chatting with my friends, creating new recipes, fermenting food, growing my plants, or dancing to new and old music (while cooking too!). I ride my bike everywhere and don't want to own a car. I love playing with my kids and answering their questions about the world. I read anything Ican get my hands on related to health, psychology, company culture, technology, business, and socio-political issues… or just anything that catches my attention.

Feel free to reach out via email at hi@juliamaribernaus.com or connect with me on Linkedin.
